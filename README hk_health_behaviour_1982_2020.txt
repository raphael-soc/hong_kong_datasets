README file for hk_health_behaviour_1982_2020.csv

Dataset for a number of health-related behaviour from 1982 to 2020. Data are from different sources, data from some years is incomplete due to large sampling errors.

For the data and more exact definitions from the Hong Kong Council of Social Service (HKCSS), visit https://www.socialindicators.org.hk/en
For the data and more exact definitions from the HKJC Centre for Suicide Research and Prevention at HKU (HKJC), visit https://csrp.hku.hk/statistics/

Variables:

adults_smoking			= adults (20+) smoking per 100,000 population aged 20 and above (HKCSS)
drug_cases			= all drug cases per 100,000 population, based on mid-year estimate of population, including cases handled by the Customs and Excise Department (HKCSS)
drug_cases_15_19			= prevalence of drug use among persons aged 15 to 19 per 100,000 population aged 15 to 19 (HKCSS)
drug_abuse			= percentage of populatino abusing restricted drugs (HKCSS)
food_poisoning_affected		= number of persons affected in food poisoning cases per 100,000 population (HKCSS)
serious_mental_health_hospitalizations	= number of persons hospitalized for serious mental illnesses per 100,000 population (HKCSS)
new_std_cases			= new STD cases per 100,000 population per year (HKCSS)
new_aids_cases			= new AIDS cases per 100,000 population per year (HKCSS)
suicide_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people (HKJC)
suicide_deaths			= total number of deaths from suicide (HKJC)
suicide_rate_male			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the male population (HKJC)
suicide_rate_female		= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the female population (HKJC)
suicide_deaths_male		= total number of deaths from suicide among the male population (HKJC)		
suicide_deaths_female		= total number of deaths from suicide among the female population (HKJC)
suicide_<15_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the population aged <15 (HKJC)
suicide_15_24_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the population aged 15 to 24 (HKJC)
suicide_25_34_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the population aged 25 to 34 (HKJC)
suicide_35_44_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the population aged 35 to 44 (HKJC)
suicide_45_54_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the population aged 45 to 54 (HKJC)
suicide_55_64_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the population aged 55 to 64 (HKJC)
suicide_65+_rate			= rate of suicides per 100,000 people among the population aged 65+ (HKJC)
suicide_<15_deaths			= total number of deaths from suicide among the population aged <15 (HKJC)
suicide_15_24_deaths		= total number of deaths from suicide among the population aged 15 to 24 (HKJC)
suicide_25_34_deaths		= total number of deaths from suicide among the population aged 25 to 34 (HKJC)
suicide_35_44_deaths		= total number of deaths from suicide among the population aged 35 to 44 (HKJC)
suicide_45_54_deaths		= total number of deaths from suicide among the population aged 45 to 54 (HKJC)
suicide_55_64_deaths		= total number of deaths from suicide among the population aged 55 to 64 (HKJC)
suicide_65+_deaths			= total number of deaths from suicide among the population aged 65+ (HKJC)
youth_suicide_rate			= number of suicides in age group 10-19 per 100,000 population (HKCSS)
